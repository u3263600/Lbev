Our Story

ABOUT LBEV

LBEV is an independent label built on one principle: nothing gets made unless someone orders it. No overstock. No waste. Every piece is manufactured specifically because you wanted it.

The Sickle Bill Polo SS-01 is our debut drop — limited to 300 units per colorway. When the window closes, it's gone. For good.

Logistics

SHIPPING POLICY

Pre-Order Timeline: Orders ship 6–8 weeks after the pre-order window closes and production is approved. You'll receive email updates at every stage.

International Shipping: We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and any applicable customs duties or taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.

Tracking: A tracking number will be emailed to you once your order has shipped from our logistics partner.

Returns

REFUND POLICY

Pre-Order Cancellations: You may cancel your pre-order within 48 hours of placing it for a full refund. After 48 hours, orders cannot be cancelled as production planning has already begun.

Defective Items: If you receive a faulty or incorrect item, contact us at lbev.brand@gmail.com within 14 days of delivery with photos. We'll make it right.

Change of Mind: As each item is made-to-order, we do not accept returns or exchanges for change of mind. Please check sizing carefully before ordering.

Data

PRIVACY POLICY

What We Collect: When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details (processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card number).

How We Use It: Your information is used solely to process and fulfil your order, and to send you order updates. We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as necessary to fulfil your order (e.g. our shipping partner).

Contact: For any privacy concerns, email us at lbev.brand@gmail.com.
